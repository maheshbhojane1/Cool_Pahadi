Our Story

A Home in the Heart of Himalaya

Tucked away in the serene Rashi Village of Uttarakhand, Cool Pahadi is not just a homestay — it is an invitation to slow down, breathe the crisp mountain air, and reconnect with nature. Our stone-and-wood abode has been lovingly crafted to honour the traditional Kumaoni architectural heritage.

Every corner tells a story of the mountains. Woven rugs, handcrafted furniture, local art on the walls — every detail is intentional. Our guests wake up to panoramic Himalayan views and the sound of birds rather than alarm clocks.

We believe in responsible tourism — supporting local artisans, farmers, and guides while offering an experience that is deeply rooted in the culture of Uttarakhand.

🧔
Deepak Bisht Host & Founder · Rashi Village ▶ YouTube: Coole Pahadi

Pahadi Thali & Kitchen

Taste the Mountains

  • 🍲
    Aloo Ke Gutke Crispy Pahadi potatoes tempered with jakhiya seeds — the quintessential Uttarakhand comfort dish.
  • 🥣
    Kafuli & Chainsu Traditional spinach curry and dal made from local grains. Served with thick mandua (finger millet) rotis.
  • 🥣
    Gahat Ki Dal Horse-gram lentils slow-cooked with mountain herbs — rich in protein and deeply warming.
  • 🍵
    Buransh & Tulsi Chai A herbal tea made from Rhododendron flowers and tulsi leaves, plucked fresh from our garden.
  • 🍯
    Local Honey & Bal Mithai Wild Pahadi honey and Kumaon's famous Bal Mithai — the sweetest way to end any meal.
